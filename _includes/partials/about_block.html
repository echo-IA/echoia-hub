About echoIA

echoIA (Enabling Cosmology with Homogenized Observations of Intrinsic Alignments) is a community effort to build a unified pipeline for intrinsic alignment research. We start by collecting public galaxy shape catalogs in one place with clean links and comparable metadata.

When licensing allows, we will host or mirror on Zenodo under an echoIA collection. Each entry tracks provenance, version, license, and citations, and always links to the original source. Otherwise, we point to the official release.

  • Curate: community-maintained index of public catalogs
  • Host/Mirror: stable DOIs on Zenodo (where permitted)
  • Standardize: comparable, high-level fields across releases
  • Credit: clear licensing & recommended citations
